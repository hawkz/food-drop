Thanks so much for your support of #feedbirmingham.

Together, during this 13-week campaign, we have:

  • collected 60,000 items (that's about 18 tonnes, with a retail value of over £56,000!),
  • raised a further £6,700 in financial donations,
  • helped 192 organisations across Birmingham, and
  • supported over 66,000 people in a time of need.

What an amazing achievement! We couldn't have done it without you.

Although #feedbirmingham has finished for now, we will start it up again as the need arises. If you want to stay in touch with the team of volunteers you can like this page on Facebook or enter your details here.

In the meantime, you can continue to support the many brilliant organisations who continue to do amazing work across the city region. Here are just a few of them...
